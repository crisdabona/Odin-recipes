Lasagna

Description

The best lasagna is the homemade one. I don't know if I've ever mentioned it before, but lasagna is one of my favorite pasta dishes. Perhaps I learned to make lasagna before cannelloni for a reason, my mother loves it.

This classic of cuisine is famous worldwide, not just in Italy. It's ideal for taking to work or school in a lunchbox and is sure to be a hit among the little ones at home. Don't go overboard with the quantity, one serving is enough. It's a quite complete recipe that will give you the energy to get through a full day.

Ingredients

    For the filling (meat Bolognese):

  • 500g of meat (a mixture of pork and beef)
  • 2 red bell peppers
  • 2 carrots
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 150g of bacon or pancetta
  • 2 large onions
  • 250g of fresh tomatoes (approximately 1 glass)
  • 250ml of white wine (approximately 200ml)
  • 100ml of extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 heaping teaspoon of dried oregano (or Provencal herbs)
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper (to taste)
